SSEF WTF

SSEF is regarded as an extremely prestigious science research competition, and heralded as the 'Olympics of Research'
And each year, about 700+ projects take part in this competition to vie for the 6 ISEF places.

However, the judging process is very unfair

Hundreds of judges, each with their own biases, standards and areas of interests are invited to judge the projects at SSEF, and each project has 6 assigned to them. Every year a lot of bullshit and horror stories are generated.

Want to share your own story?

This is not meant to be a salt mine, a rage fest or a toxic chamber.
We want to see if by gathering feedback from participants, we can make SSEF a fairer fair
